Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

At IFRS Coach, we respect your right to control your data. In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety policies, you may request the complete deletion of your account and associated data.

What data will be deleted?

When your deletion request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Account Credentials: Your registered email and encrypted password.
  • User Profile: Your name, avatar, and language preferences.
  • Learning Progress: All course progress, bookmarks, and quiz scores.
  • Personal Notes: Any notes you have saved within the application.

Note: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

How to Request Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Instant)

If you still have the app installed and can log in:

  1. Open the IFRS Coach app.
  2. Go to Profile / Settings.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice. Your data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Web Request (If you cannot access the app)

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot log in, you may request deletion manually by contacting our support team. We will process your request within 30 days.

Please click the button below to generate a deletion request email:

Request Account Deletion via Email

Alternatively, you can email us directly at support@ifrsapp.zohodesk.eu with the subject line "Delete Account". Please include the email address used to register the account.
